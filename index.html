Why Study ग्राम

Coaching that stays close to home — and close to the syllabus.

Study ग्राम was founded on 5 July 2026 with a simple belief: students from Bhadohi Road and nearby villages deserve the same quality of exam preparation as students in the city, without the long commute.

  • Small batches, so no student goes unnoticed.

  • Parents receive performance reports, not just report cards.
